Reading the display

The following notation is used:

  • O, #, T, * are circles, squares, triangle, and stars, respectively.
  • Dots are empty cells.
  • Round parentheses () surround pieces that can be moved presently.
  • Square bracket surround the cell to which the last MOVE was applied; the cell will be empty [.] if the move was successful (the piece has been removed), or non-empty, e.g. [O], if the move was rejected.
